How It Works

Three-stage AI pipeline that mirrors what real ATS systems do — and more.

01

Parse & Chunk

Resume is parsed from PDF/DOCX and split into semantically meaningful chunks for granular analysis.

02

Embed & Retrieve (RAG)

Sentence-Transformers encode every chunk into vectors stored in FAISS. The job description retrieves the most relevant resume sections.

03

LLM Analysis

Gemini 2.5 Flash receives the RAG context and generates deep insights: ATS score, gaps, strengths, and a personalized action plan.
